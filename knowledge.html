General Knowledge for Farmers

  • Soil Health: Regularly test soil pH and nutrients. Rotate crops to maintain fertility.
  • Water Management: Use drip irrigation for efficiency and harvest rainwater to save resources.
  • Crop Selection: Choose climate-appropriate and high-yield varieties.
  • Pest Control: Practice Integrated Pest Management (IPM) with natural and chemical methods.
  • Livestock Care: Provide balanced diets, vaccinations, and weather-proof shelters.
  • Sustainable Practices: Try agroforestry and renewable energy solutions for long-term benefits.
  • Technology: Leverage mobile apps, sensors, and drones for precision farming.
  • Market Knowledge: Stay updated on crop demands and sell directly to consumers when possible.
  • Climate Adaptation: Use drought-resistant crops, mulching, and crop insurance.
